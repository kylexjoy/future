About Us

We’re Dedicated to Flawless Construction of Exacting Design. The men and women of Modern Art Inc. are drawn to construction as a tradition of craft, and honored to be part of its evolution. Whether creating mortise and tenon joinery for a piece of custom millwork or high-performance wall assemblies for a cutting-edge green building, our carpenters’ zeal for precision allows the shared vision of client and architect to be fully realized and well-built.
